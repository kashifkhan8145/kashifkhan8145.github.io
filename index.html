About

With a background in ML and signal modeling, I apply modern ML techniques to solve real‑world RF problems with measurable impact. My work spans surrogate modelling for GaN HEMTs, microstrip filter synthesis, and metaheuristic optimization (GWO, PSO, WOA, ALO, BHO, RSA, SHO). I value interpretability, careful evaluation, and robust deployment.
